Homemade Cappuccino

Ingredients

  • 400 g of Powdered Milk
  • 2 Cups of Chocolate Powder 50% Cocoa (240 ml Cup)
  • 100 g of Instant Coffee
  • 1 Tablespoon of Baking Soda
  • 1 Teaspoon of Cinnamon Powde
  • (To Serve)100 ml of Hot Milk or Water

Modo de Fazer (How to Make)

Start by adding the powdered milk in a food processor. If you don't have a processor at home, you can make this recipe in a blender, and it will work out just fine. Add the chocolate powder and the instant coffee. We used extra strong coffee, but if you like a milder taste, use a little less. Add the baking soda, the cinnamon powder, but cinnamon is optional, and beat everything very well until combined. Next, transfer to a jar that has an airtight lid to seal it well and preserve the flavor of the recipe. Now, to serve, add 3 teaspoons of the cappuccino powder in very hot milk and mix. And if you like a milder flavor, you can replace the milk with very hot water. Serve.
